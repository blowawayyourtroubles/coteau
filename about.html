About Cocteau's Tea

The art of blending, The joy of discovery, The appreciation of subtle nuances

At Cocteau's Tea, we believe in the transformative power of tea. Each cup is a journey, steeped in history, culture, and craftsmanship. Inspired by the boundless creativity of Jean Cocteau and the timeless elegance of Yves Saint Laurent, we've created more than just a store—we've created a haven.

Our carefully curated selection brings together rare blends, sustainable sourcing, and the artistry of tea-making. Every leaf tells a story, every sip evokes a memory. Whether you're discovering a new favorite or returning to a beloved classic, we're here to guide you.

Cocteau's Tea is a celebration of connection. It's where art meets tradition, where elegance meets comfort, and where tea enthusiasts gather to share in the ritual of tea.

Welcome to Cocteau's Tea—a world where every cup is extraordinary.

Our Mission

At Cocteau's Tea, we're on a mission to elevate the tea experience. We believe that tea is more than just a beverage—it's a way of life. Our mission is to share the artistry, history, and health benefits of tea with the world. We're committed to sourcing the finest teas, supporting sustainable practices, and creating a welcoming space for tea lovers of all kinds.
